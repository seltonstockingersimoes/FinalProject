What Are Ethical Issues in Web Development?

Ethical web development means creating websites that respect users and provide honest and fair experiences. Developers SHOULD AND HAVE a responsibility to avoid harm and build technology that benefits people rather than exploiting them. AKA, please don't steal from your users.

Common Ethical Issues

Privacy and Data Collection

Many websites collect personal data. Ethical developers ensure transparency, ask for consent, and protect user information instead of misusing it.

Misinformation

Developers should avoid spreading false or misleading information, please don't spread fake news. Websites should present good and reliable, high-quality content.
