What Are Ethical Issues in Web Development?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is transforming how websites are built and used (for good or bad.) Developers can use AI tools to generate code, improve user experience and automate repetitive tasks.

Advantages of AI

  • Faster development process
  • Improved user experience
  • Better decision-making with data.
  • Automation of repetitive tasks are better and more effective.

Challenges and Problems

  • Privacy and data security risks
  • Over-reliance on AI tools
  • Half of the time the AI is just copying information, not actually understanding.
  • Ethical concerns and bias
  • Possible job displacement
